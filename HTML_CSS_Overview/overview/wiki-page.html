The Brown Bear

The brown bear (Ursus arctos) is a large bear species found across Eurasia and North America.In North America, the populations of brown bears are called grizzly bears, while the subspecies that inhabits the Kodiak Islands of Alaska is known as the Kodiak bear. It is one of the largest living terrestrial members of the order Carnivora, rivaled in size only by its closest relative, the polar bear (Ursus maritimus).The brown bear is recognized as a national and state animal in several European countries
